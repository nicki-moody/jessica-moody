Peacebuilding

My peacebuilding work is research focused and entails helping NGOs, charities and think tanks to better understand the environments in which they work and the opportunities for peace that exist in conflict and post-conflict countries. To do this I undertake extensive desk- and field-based research and analyse large datasets regarding conflict trends, as well as perceptions of civilians and powerbrokers in a given country. I have experience designing methodologies, surveys and research questions, as well as harvesting, cleaning and processing both qualitative and quantitative data. I have worked both alone and in teams on peacebuilding research projects and have extensive project management experience in this area as well.

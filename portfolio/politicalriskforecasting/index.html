Politican Risk Forecasting

For my political risk work I conduct extensive open source research, as well as interviews with contacts I have across West and Central Africa in the military, government and among civil society. Through these conversations and the research that I do, I am able to expertly analyse events and accurately forecast likely political, security and regulatory changes. This advice helps investors to better plan for forthcoming challenges. Some of my work in this area can be found below.
